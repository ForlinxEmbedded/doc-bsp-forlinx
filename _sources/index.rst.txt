Welcome to Forlinx Embedded's Documentation!
=============================================

Forlinx Embedded is a leading embedded solution provider specializing in ARM-based SoMs and industrial-grade single board computers.  
We support a wide range of platforms such as Rockchip, NXP i.MX6/i.MX8/i.MX9, and TI Sitara, aiming to empower industrial automation, robotics, medical, and IoT applications.
